Features

Free

Share the Ride is a free platform, developed with one goal in mind: clean, faster ride booking.

Reduced Spam

By controlling the number of posts a driver can post, the platform will help maintain a clean interface preventing any duplicates.

All information at one place

Riders are required to post all the information about the ride upfront, so neither the driver or rider waste their time on repetitive conversations.
